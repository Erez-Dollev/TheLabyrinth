TheLabyrinth is a git repository, an online provider, that serves a library of books, including this one.
These books are content provided to the ["SummeryTree","Instructions/SummeryTree"] app.

"One can think of The Labyrinth as a well that waters the roots of the Summery Tree".

By ["contacting","Instructions/TheLabyrinth/Contact.txt"] the master of the labyrith, you can add or edit books.
